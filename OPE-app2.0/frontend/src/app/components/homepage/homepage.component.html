About OPE

Our thesis software is designed to help buyers and sellers make informed decisions about products based on customer feedback. Using innovative approaches such as Naive Bayes and Aspect Extraction models, OPE analyzes customer reviews to identify the aspects that matter the most and the sentiment associated with each aspect.

How it Works

We use Naive Bayes and Aspect Extraction models to analyze customer reviews of products. These models help us identify the aspects that customers care about the most and the sentiment associated with each aspect. Based on this information, we provide a comprehensive evaluation of the product that includes a breakdown of its strengths and weaknesses.
